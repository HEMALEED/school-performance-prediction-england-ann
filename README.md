# Predicting School Performance in England Using Artificial Neural Networks: A Socio-Economic Perspective

## Research Aim

This MRes research investigates how socio-economic, institutional, academic, behavioural, and wellness factors influence school performance in England.

The study develops an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) model to predict school performance outcomes using integrated datasets from:

* Department for Education (DfE)
* Office for National Statistics (ONS)
* Ofsted
* Index of Multiple Deprivation (IMD)

Explainable AI techniques including SHAP and LIME will be applied to improve model interpretability and identify the most influential factors affecting school performance.

## Research Objectives

1. Identify key socio-economic indicators affecting school performance.
2. Integrate DfE, Ofsted, and ONS datasets.
3. Develop and evaluate ANN models.
4. Apply SHAP and LIME explainability techniques.
5. Generate evidence-based policy insights.
